Asking Questions:
  - If you can google the question, google it.
  - If you can’t, ask “I am having trouble with [X]. This is what I currently understand about the problem. [Y]. This is what I’ve tried before to solve the problem: [Z]. How should I achieve [X]?

For interviews, some tips are:
  - Ask how to prepare for the interview before the interview.
  - Don’t include a project on your resume if you don’t remember it.
  - Learn public speaking skills and communicate well
